Use Bitcoin safety.

Bitcoin transactions are relatively anonyous which can lead to disreputable people stealing your coins. Take the risk out of transacting with Bitcoin by allowing us to oversee your transaction.

Cryptographic voting.

We never have access to your funds, all we have is one vote in a cryptographic M of N transaction. Basically, this means that if both you and the other person reach agreement the Bitcoins can be released. In the event of disagreeemnt we can cast our vote to release or refund the Bitcoins.

Designed to be easy to use.
